## Archive Principles

1. **Archive after replacement** - Only move files here after their replacement is confirmed working
2. **Keep for reference** - Don't delete immediately - you may need to reference old formats
3. **Organize by date** - Consider adding date prefixes to make sorting easier
4. **Compress old files** - Very old files can be zipped to save space

## Cleanup Schedule

- **After 1 semester:** Review archive, delete clearly obsolete files
- **After 1 year:** Compress all files older than 1 year into `.zip` archives
- **Before exams:** Never clean archive during exam prep period

### 3. TRESPASS & RIGHT TO EXCLUDE

**Best source:** `Professor Lee, Property Spring 2024.txt` (lines 370-566)

**Sub-topics:**

#### 3a. Trespass to Land (lines 370-403)
- *Jacque v. Steenberg Homes* → absolute right to exclude, punitive damages
- *Hinman v. Pacific Air Transport* → Ad Coelum rejected for airspace

#### 3b. Continual/Repeated Trespass (lines 404-420)
- *Baker v. Howard County Hunt* → BAKER 2-PART TEST for equitable relief

#### 3c. Continuous/Uninterrupted Trespass (lines 421-465)
- *Pile v. Pedrick* vs. *Golden Press v. Rylands*
- MODERN RULE: 3-PART BALANCING TEST for good faith encroachments

#### 3d. Enforcement (lines 466-527)
- Criminal vs. civil enforcement
- Self-help: *Berg v. Wiley* (real property), *Williams v. Ford* (movable property)

#### 3e. Limits on Right to Exclude (lines 528-635)
- Necessity (*Ploof v. Putnam*)
- Public accommodations (*Uston v. Resorts*)
- Public policy (*State v. Shack*)
- Anti-discrimination (*Shelley v. Kraemer*, Fair Housing Act)

**2021 outline:** Lines 1200-1600 (more detailed trespass doctrine)

## Exam Format Evolution

| Period | Format | Time | Questions | Style |
|--------|--------|------|-----------|-------|
| 2020-2025 | Take-home | 4-8 hrs | 3 | Issue-spotter + policy + reflective |
| 2015-2019 | Take-home | 4-6 hrs | 3 | Same pattern |
| 2002-2014 | Varies | 3-4 hrs | 2-3 | Sometimes in-person |

**Consistent Pattern (Harcourt):**
1. **Q1:** Issue-spotter fact pattern — apply MPC as prosecutor AND defense
2. **Q2:** Policy essay — synthesize theory readings, original argument
3. **Q3:** Reflective essay — professional/ethical reflection
